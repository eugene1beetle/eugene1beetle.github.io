Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how PlaneCater ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about what data we access and why.

1. Information We Access

The App requests access to the following device features:

  • Camera — used solely to allow you to take a photo for your profile picture. We do not record video, stream camera data, or use the camera for any other purpose.
  • Photo Library / Gallery — used solely to allow you to choose an existing photo from your device as your profile picture. We do not scan, read, or upload any other photos from your gallery.

2. How We Use the Information

Photos you capture or select are used only to set your in-app profile picture. The image may be uploaded to our servers to display your profile picture within the App. We do not use your photos for advertising, analytics, or any purpose beyond displaying your profile.

3. Data Storage and Security

Profile pictures you upload are stored securely on our servers. We use industry-standard measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss. We retain your profile picture for as long as your account is active. You can remove or replace your profile picture at any time from within the App.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We do not share your photos or profile data with any third party except as required by law or to provide core App functionality (e.g., cloud storage providers acting as data processors on our behalf).

5. Permissions Summary

  • android.permission.CAMERA – take a profile photo in-app
  • READ_MEDIA_IMAGES / READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE – select a profile photo from gallery

These permissions are only used when you actively choose to set or update your profile picture. You can revoke these permissions at any time in your device Settings → Apps → PlaneCater → Permissions.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
